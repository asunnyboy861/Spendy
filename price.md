# Pricing Configuration

## Monetization Model: Free

- **Price**: Free (No charge)
- **IAP**: Not required
- **Subscription**: None

## App Store Connect Pricing
- **Price Tier**: Free

## Policy Pages Required
- Support Page: ✅
- Privacy Policy: ✅
- Terms of Use: ❌ (Not needed for free apps)

## Rationale
Spendy is positioned as a free, privacy-first expense tracker to differentiate from expensive competitors like YNAB ($109/year), Monarch Money ($14.99/month), and EveryDollar ($17.99/month). The "completely free" positioning is a core competitive advantage that removes all barriers to entry and aligns with the app's philosophy of simplicity. Future monetization may include a premium tier with advanced features, but the core tracking experience remains free.
